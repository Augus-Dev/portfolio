Chocolate Salami

Description

Chocolate salami is a traditional no-bake dessert made with dark chocolate, butter, sugar, and crushed biscuits. It is shaped like a salami, sliced, and served as a sweet treat.

This recipe is simple, quick to prepare, and a fun dessert to share with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 200 g dark chocolate
  • 100 g butter
  • 150 g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 g digestive biscuits (or tea cookies), crushed
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon rum or milk (optional)
  • Powdered sugar (for coating)

Steps

  1. Melt the dark chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water.
  2. Whisk in the sugar, then add the eggs (lightly beaten).
  3. Stir in the crushed biscuits, cocoa powder, and optional rum or milk.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a sheet of parchment paper and shape it into a log.
  5. Wrap tightly and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, until firm.
  6. Before serving, roll the log in powdered sugar to give it the appearance of salami.
  7. Slice into discs and enjoy.
