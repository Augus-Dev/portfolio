Vegetable Stir-fry

Description

Quick, easy, and healthy! This vegetable stir-fry combines crisp, colorful vegetables with a savory sauce for a delicious meal that can be served with rice or noodles.

The secret to a great stir-fry is high heat, constant tossing, and a simple, balanced sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 100 g snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Cooked rice or noodles (to serve)

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and onion, stir-fry for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrot, broccoli, and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Toss in the snap peas and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir well to coat the vegetables.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
