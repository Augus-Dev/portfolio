Lasagne alla bolognese

Description

One of the most loved foods in the world is here! If you follow the recipe you can create a beautiful Italian Lasagna with layers of slow cooked Ragù Bolognese and Besciamella cheese sauce.

The secret is love, patient and also passion for Lego. See. You just have to cook separate dishes and put them all together:

  1. Cook the meat sauce, known as Ragú;
  2. Prepare the besciamella sauce;
  3. Put all in layers and take it to the oven.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil, butter, onion, garlic, carrot and celery – for the flavour base, a sofrito;
  • Panceta bacon;
  • Beef;
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste;
  • Red wine;
  • Seasonings: Salt, black pepper, basil, peppers, thyme, rosemary;
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • White pepper and nutmeg
  • Parmigianno cheese
  • Lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Put in the pan the sofrito, then the bacon, then the chopped vegetables and then the meat. Deglaze with wine and let it cook
  2. Add the tomato sauce and the seasonings. Cook for 20-40 minutes
  3. Prepare the beasciamella sauce
  4. Build the layers: Lasagna sheets, ragú, besciamella and cheese. Repeat
  5. Take to the oven for 40 minutes
